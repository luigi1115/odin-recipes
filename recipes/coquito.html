Coquito Recipe

Description

No, this is not eggnog, this is COQUITO!

A traditional christmas drink from Puerto Rico to enjoy with your families and friends on the happiest holidays of the year.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 can of evaporated milk
  • 1 can of cream of coconut
  • 1/2 cup of white rum (Preferably Don Q or Bacardi)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon (add more to taste)

Steps

  1. In a blender, add evaporated milk, cream of coconut, sweetened condensed milk, rum, vanilla extract, and cinnamon.
  2. Blend on high until mixture is well combined for 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Transfer mixture into glass bottles and chill in the refrigerator until cold.
  4. To serve, pour coquito into small serving glasses (almost like a shot portion) and garnish with ground cinnamon or a cinnamon stick.
